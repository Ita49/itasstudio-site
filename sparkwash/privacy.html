SparkWash

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

SparkWash is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. Who we are

SparkWash is a mobile car valeting service operating in the Wellingborough area of Northamptonshire, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal information collected through the SparkWash mobile application.

Contact: support@itasstudio.com

2. What data we collect

When you use SparkWash, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored securely and never in plain text)
  • Contact details — your mobile phone number
  • Address information — your home or service address for booking purposes
  • Booking details — the services you book, dates, times, and payment method
  • Payment information — payment method preference (card or cash). Card payments are processed by Stripe and we do not store your card details directly
  • Device information — push notification token to send you booking alerts and reminders
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, to improve our service

3. Why we collect it

We collect and use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your SparkWash account
  • To process and fulfil your bookings
  • To contact you about your appointment (reminders, confirmations, operator updates)
  • To send you push notifications related to your bookings
  • To process payments securely
  • To improve and maintain the SparkWash service
  • To comply with our legal obligations

Our legal basis for processing is contract performance (to fulfil your booking) and legitimate interests (to improve our service and communicate with you about your appointments).

4. How we store your data

Your personal data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Supabase servers are located within the European Union and comply with GDPR requirements.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the following trusted third parties, strictly to provide our service:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication provider
  • Stripe — payment processing (when card payments are enabled). Stripe is PCI DSS compliant and does not share your data with us beyond transaction confirmation
  • Expo (Meta) — push notification delivery infrastructure
  • SparkWash operators — your name, address, and booking details are shared with the assigned operator to fulfil your booking

We will never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

6. Push notifications

If you allow notifications, we send you booking confirmations, reminders, and status updates. You can turn these off at any time in your device Settings → Notifications → SparkWash.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@itasstudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

The SparkWash mobile app does not use cookies. Our web presence may use essential cookies only, which are necessary for the site to function.

9. Children's privacy

SparkWash is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact and complaints

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

Email: support@itasstudio.com

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):
